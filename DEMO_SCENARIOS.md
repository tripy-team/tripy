## Pro Tips for Demos

### Dates
- **Always pick dates 2-4 weeks in the future** - better flight availability
- **Avoid holidays** - pricing gets weird, harder to predict
- **Mid-week departures** (Tue/Wed) often have better award availability

### Budget
- **Domestic round-trip**: $400-800
- **Transatlantic round-trip**: $2,000-4,000
- **Transpacific round-trip**: $2,000-3,500
- **Setting budget too low** will show the budget adjustment suggestions (also a good demo!)

### Points
- **Chase and Amex are the most flexible** - they transfer to the most partners
- **100k points** is a good baseline for domestic
- **200k+ points** for international business class

### Cities to Mention in Chatbot
The natural language parser handles these well:
- "I want to go to Paris and London"
- "NYC to Tokyo"
- "LA to Miami, then back"
- "Chicago to Rome for 2 weeks"

### Airport Codes That Work Best
```
US Hubs: JFK, LAX, SFO, ORD, MIA, FLL, DFW, ATL, SEA, BOS
Europe: LHR, CDG, AMS, FRA, FCO, BCN, MAD
Asia: NRT, HND, ICN, SIN, HKG, BKK
```
